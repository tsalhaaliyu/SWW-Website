INTRODCTION OF CORDON SEARCH

Cordon and search is normally a combined military and police security operation in a counter revolutionary operation, when tasked to restore law and order. Although it is the work of police to search but the military come in there is an uprising or revolutionary upset. Based on good intelligence, it should be preplanned in detail and rehearsed also the place should be Recce. The plan should be simple and capable of rapid execution.

DEFINITION

Cordon and search is a combined military and police operation aimed at arresting a key person, material, equipment or document.

REASONS FOR CORDON AND SEARCH

The following are reasons for cordon and search.

  1. To arrest suspect e.g kidnapper, drug dealer or other law breakers.
  2. To recover confiscated materials e.g IED
  3. To recover weapons and other equipment.

PRINCIPLES OF CORDON AND SEARCH

The success of cordon and search operation depends on the following principles:

  1. Secrecy.
    • Secrecy. All information must be kept secret. Recce of the general area must be in civil cloth to prevent disclosing the plan to the enemy.
  2. Surprise.
    • Surprise. All surprise must be achieve in a conceal deployments.
  3. Intelligence.
    • Intelligence. Include the information of the general layout, the habit, plans, tactics, arms and ammunition.
  4. Simple Plan.
    • Simple Plan. All plans must be made and understood by the troops.
  5. Time factor.
    • Time factor. There must be time appreciation so as not to have a premature time to strike.
    • Detailed planning and rehearsalT
    • horough briefing of all troops taking part in the operation.

THE TASK OF THE MILITARY

  1. Provision of cordon.
  2. Escorting police searchers.
  3. Guarding detained suspect.
  4. Providing helicopter if required and available to spoor movement within the cordon area.
  5. Providing technical assistance for searchers e.g mine detectors etc.

THE TASK OF THE POLICE

  1. Decide the areas to be searched by the intelligence network.
  2. Carry out close recce before the search in a plan cloth.
  3. Warn the locals by imposing curfew where the search will take place.
  4. Conducting the search and arresting the suspects.
  5. Securing the suspects.
  6. Proving guidelines to the military guards.
  7. Control traffic.

ORGANIZATION OF CORDON AND SEARCH

The following parties are involved in cordon and search:

  1. Cordon troops – military.
  2. Search party – police/military.
  3. Escort party – military.
  4. Cage party – police/military.
  5. Screening team – police.
  6. Road block team –military/police.
  7. Reserve team – military/police.

CORDON TROOPS

The cordon troops are sub divided into two groups:

  1. Outer Cordon
    2. The Outer Cordon. These are the troops that provide the outer security, they prevent the escape of the suspects and possible reinforcement. They also prevent people entering the search area from outside.
  2. Inner Cordon
    3. The Inner Cordon. These are the troops that provide the inner security. They also prevent the escape of the suspect from the inner cordoned area.
  3. SEARCH PARTY
    4. These are the ones that carryout the search. They identify the suspect, document or anything needed. This party consists of the police and troops in support
  4. ESCORT ARTY
    5. These are the ones that escort the suspects to the cage where they will undergo screening and detention. This party comprise of the military.
  5. CAGE PARTY
    6. These are the ones that provide security at the cage guide the suspects and the detainees. They are made up of the military.
  6. SCREENING PARTY
    7. These are responsible for screening the arrested suspects, they comprise the police, intelligent, informants.
  7. ROAD BLOCK PARTY
    8. They are the party that prevent escape of suspect through exists. They control traffic and prevent reinforcement and entry into the search area. They also search the vehicles. This is made up of the police and the military.
  8. RESERVE PARTY
    9. These are the ones that provide necessary reinforcement and can also act as casualty evacuation. They also provide roof top sentries, moment operation for early warning. They comprises of the police and the military.

SEQUENCE OF CORDON AND SEARCH

The sequence of carrying out cordon and search operation are:

  1. Intelligence.
  2. Recce.
  3. Planning.
  4. Movement to R.V.
  5. Deployment of outer cordon.
  6. Curlew impose.
  • INTELLIGENCE
    • Proper intelligent work will be carried out in order to provide information on the general layout and town plan of the area to be searched after cordoning
  • RECCE
    • To carry out proper investigation, to know the habits of the populace, location, strength, arms, and ammunition. Also to know whether there is dogs in the area. After the plain cloth recce all information received will be properly disseminated to the military/police joints ops room.
  • PLANNING
    • For surprise to be achieved these must be sound planning. Rehearsal must follow after putting into consideration the mode of transportation (either foot or else). Timings also must be made so as to know when to carry out each stage.
  • MOVEMENT TO RV
    • After planning and rehearsals proper tactical movement from the base or the RV must be made and will lead to deployment.
  • DEPLOYMENT OF OUTER CORDON
    • The outer cordon and road block party must be effectively deployed before the announcement of curfew so as to prevent suspect from escaping

    >SEARCHING, ARRESTING, RECOVERY, AND SCREENING

    Proper screening must be carried out and carefully done so as to avoid isolating a practical bound. Recovered document, arms and ammunition or suspect must be handed over to the cadge party for further interrogation and screening

      LOW LEVEL SEARCH SCREENING
       This is done during the search and it is aimed to eliminate persons of good character and pick up suspects for a higher level screening at the central cage. This is carried out by the local police or representatives of the branch.
      HIGH LEVEL SEARCH SCREENING
      This is done by the police at the central cage. The aim is to interrogate all suspected person pick up during the search and to decide who should be detained. Although these screening is the responsibilities of the police troops will have escort the suspected person to the central cadge and keep them under observation while they may wait their turn for interrogation.

    DIFFICULTIES IN CORDON AND SEARCH

    The success of cordon and search depends mostly on the commander and the number of men involved. Cordon and search require manpower therefore there is need to ensure there is enough men to carryout the operation.
