COUNTER REVOLUTIONARY WARFARE (CRW) STANDARD TERMS

INTRODUCTION

REVOLUTIONARY WARFARE is a process which the use of political economic and military measure that terrorist working mainly within the country employ to weaken and overthrow the government. It’s a military measure which include sabotage, terrorism and insurgency with the accompaniment of guerrilla warfare.

Revolutionary war was originally a communist term and apart from that it is many part of the world similar method, it has been and is likely to be use by different groups who are active as alternative power leaders.

STANDARD TERMS DEFINATION OF CRW

  1. REVOLUTION:-is defined as violence means or non violence means of change of government policy.
  2. COUNTER REVOLUTION:- The measure put in place to prevent, stop or deter any revolution or violence.
  3. TERRORIST TARGET :- Target of violence, Target of interest, Target of influence.
  4. REBELLION:- It’s an armed expression of dissatisfaction of government policy/activities or others.
  5. INSURGENCY:- It’s any formed of rebellion in which a dissident factor that has a support of substantial part of the population indelicate the
    commission of wide separate of civil disobedient sabotage and terrorism to wedged a guerilla warfare in order to overthrown the government.

TYPES OF REVOLUTION

  1. Violence Revolution
  2. Non-violence Revolution
    • VIOLENCE REVOLUTION: revolution that involves the use of violence as a means of expression e.g Violence protest, insurgency, nobelium riot, terrorism.
    • NON –VIOLENCE REVOLUTION: revolution expressed without the use of violence means e.g peaceful Protest, propaganda, strike

CAUSES OF UNREST

  1. Injustice
  2. Illiteracy
  3. Unemployment
  4. Political
  5. Religious differences
  6. Border dispute

BASIC METHOD OF REVOLUTION

  1. Destructive method
    • Destructive method: its an act of attacking the established order and its supporters e.g.
    • Anti-government propaganda and demoralization
    • Full scale military operation
    • Guerrilla operation
    • Intimidation, blackmail, terrorism and assassination
  2. CONSTRUCTIVE METHOD
    • Constructive Method: its an act of building up the will to fight the means fighting the alternative government structure and organization e.g.
    • Expansion of a live political organization
    • The building up of insurgent training cadres
    • Creation of policy and military forces in preparation for the take-over and control
    • Creation of an alternative administration parallel at all levels to the government administration.

CHARACTERISTICS OF REVOLUTION

  1. It is primarily an internal struggle without front areas and is often covertly assisted.
  • It is deeply concerned with the aspiration of the people.
