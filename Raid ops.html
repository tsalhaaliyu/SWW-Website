RAID MISSION/OPERATIONS

INTRODUCTION

Raiding also known as depredation is a special forces operational warfare mission which has a purpose. Raider do not capture and hold a location but
quickly retreat to a previous RV before adversaries can re-organize to form a counter attack. It demoralizes the enemy thereby increases troops moral.

AIM

The aim of this lesson is to teach the soldier how to carry out Raid operation

    2. Raid is a sudden blow, struck with maximum force, on a pin point target, followed with swift disengagement and rapid withdrawal with no

REASON FOR RAID MISSION

  1. To capture or destroy a specific enemy material
  2. To destroy enemy head quarters, paralyze the command and control of the enemy and also create panic by the following actions.
  3. Kill enemy commander and their principal staff officers
  4. Inflict maximum causality on them
  5. Destroy enemy communication and command post.
  6. Destroy enemy equepment and capture some field
  7. Destroy their communication lines
  8. Neutralize gun area in conjunction with launching of own attack.

FACTORS AFFECTING CONDUCT RAID MISSION

  1. Morale: The morale of the raiding troops is the most important factor of a raid mission.
    2. The moral is affected by the following
    • Leadership: is all round efficiency, sound planning and physical fitness.
    • Endurance: You most get both physical and mental endurance
    • Alertness: You must be on alert at all times.
    • Efficiency: This is the ability for an individual to do more than one persons job e.g helping your friends in the jungle.
    • Good administration: Ration must be made available and also well distributed to troops under command.

  2. TARGET SELECTION

  3. The raiding troops may given a particular target to hit e.g enemy headquarters, enemy medical centre, bridges e.t.c with the aim of weakening the enemy.
  4. Point to consider when selecting a target.
    • Usefulness of the target to the enemy
    • Accessibility: how close you can move to the enemy.
    • Vulnerability: try to know the vulnerable point to strike
    • Vulnerability: try to know the vulnerable point to strike,general lay out of the area
    • the size, measurement, location, height, surface, etc.
    • defensive and security arrangement by the enemy
    • enemy disposition and activities and possible hideout

  5. SOUND PLANNING

    6. Raid operation require meticulous planning up to the last minute detail because a minor error jeopardize the operation, 100% information may
    not be achieved, therefore calculated risks should be taking. Security of plan and personnel is considered
  6. Surprise
    7. the raiding force should conceal their self when approaching the enemy territory.
  7. field craft
    8. the raiding force should have a standard knowledge of field craft.

GROUPING AND TASKING

The following are groups involve in raid operation
  • . Raid HQ: their tasks is to remain in the headquarter with some other troops
  • Assault group: Their task is to assault the enemy building.
  • Sentry silencing party: Their task is to silent enemy sentry.
  • Specialist group: Their tasks is to detonent enemy bombs or mines.
  • Protection party left and right: Their attacks is to protect their troops from enemy infiltration.
  • . Cut-off group left and right: Their task is to cut enemy
  • . Covering group
  • Reserve: Their task is to make sure their back is well safe.
